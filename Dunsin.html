Welcome to the Aviation Community Group

We are a group of aviation enthusiasts who are passionate about flying. We offer a variety of resources and services to help our members stay up-to-date on the latest aviation news, learn new skills, and connect with other aviation enthusiasts.

Here are some of the things you can do as a member of the Aviation Community Group:

  • Read our blog for the latest aviation news and information.
  • Watch our videos for tutorials on how to fly different types of aircraft.
  • Join our forums to discuss aviation with other enthusiasts.
  • Attend our events to meet other aviation enthusiasts and learn new skills.

If you are interested in joining the Aviation Community Group, please visit our website and sign up for an account.
